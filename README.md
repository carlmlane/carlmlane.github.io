# Carl M. Lane

I'm a VP of Engineering & Product Development at [Marqii](https://www.marqii.com), based in San Francisco with my wife and our little one. I've been writing code since 1998 and building engineering teams for most of my career.

This is [carlmlane.com](https://carlmlane.com) — my personal corner of the internet. It's a place where I share a bit about who I am, what I believe in, and the people who inspire me.

## My story

I was born in the Bay Area, lived in Madrid after high school, and studied aerospace engineering at SLU Parks College. I spent nearly a decade at Wix.com before landing at Marqii. My path from support rep to VP taught me that the best engineering leaders never lose touch with the people they build for.

On [carlmlane.com](https://carlmlane.com) I share more about my journey, the principles I lead by, and the people who inspire me — from Marie Curie and Grace Hopper to Linus Torvalds and Steve Wozniak.

## How I lead

> Build customer-obsessed teams that own outcomes, not tickets. Hire for curiosity, invest in growth, and create the conditions where people are inspired to do their best work.

- Small, high-IQ teams beat large, bureaucratic ones every time
- Engineering is a business lever, not a cost center
- Speed is the only sustainable competitive advantage
- Sincere enthusiasm is contagious
